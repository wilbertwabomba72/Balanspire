Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Balanspire is a casual physics stacking game. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Store On Your Device

Balanspire stores gameplay preferences and progress locally on your device, including completed levels, star ratings, the last selected level, music settings, and sound settings. This information is used only to run the game experience and is not sold by us.

Information We Do Not Require

Balanspire does not require you to create an account. The game does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access.

Rewarded Advertising

Balanspire may offer rewarded ads after certain failed attempts so that you can continue a level. Ads may be provided by third-party advertising partners. These partners may process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, ad interaction data, and other technical information to deliver, measure, and limit advertising. Their processing is governed by their own privacy policies and applicable consent requirements.

Analytics

Balanspire does not use a separate analytics account system. Basic technical events may be processed by advertising partners when rewarded ads are loaded, displayed, or completed.

Children's Privacy

Balanspire is designed as a general-audience casual game. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service connected to the app, please contact the relevant service provider or the developer through the App Store product page.

Data Retention

Local game progress and settings remain on your device until you delete the app or clear the app's stored data. Data handled by third-party advertising partners is retained according to their own policies.

Your Choices

  • You can turn music and sound effects on or off inside the game settings.
  • You can avoid rewarded ads by choosing not to use the revive option.
  • You can limit ad tracking through your device privacy settings where available.
  • You can delete local game data by deleting the app from your device.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes or when legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the developer through the App Store product page for Balanspire.
